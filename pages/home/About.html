About the Department of Computer Science

Our Mission

The Department of Computer Science, Federal Polytechnic Bauchi is dedicated to preserving, disseminating, and expanding the body of knowledge in computation. Our mission is to become a leading center for interdisciplinary research in computing, promoting scientific discoveries, technological innovation, entrepreneurship, and industrial collaborations.

We are committed to the values of inclusivity, integrity, openness, and collegiality. Our department serves as a hub for interdisciplinary research, collaborating with other departments within our institution. Our research areas include digital technology, AI, Cybersecurity, Software and Web development, and Networking and Cloud Computing.

Student Success

At the heart of our mission is a steadfast commitment to student success. We strive to provide a supportive and enriching environment where students can thrive academically and personally. Our programs are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computer science. We offer numerous opportunities for hands-on learning, internships, and collaborations with industry leaders, ensuring our students are well-prepared for their future careers.

Join us as we strive to make a local and international impact through our commitment to excellence in computing sciences and our unwavering dedication to student success.
